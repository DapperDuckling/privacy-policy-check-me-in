# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our extensions handle user data. We are committed to a "Privacy by Design" approach, ensuring that your data remains under your control.

---

## 1. Overview
The Extension is a productivity tool designed to enhance your browsing experience. Our core philosophy is that your data belongs to you.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
* **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or physical location to external servers.
* **Local Storage:** Any settings, configurations, or user-generated content are stored locally within your browser using the `chrome.storage` API. This data never leaves your device.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, web beacons, or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) to track your browsing history or behavior.

## 3. Permissions
The Extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function, here are some:
* **Storage:** Used to save your user preferences and settings locally.
* **Host Permissions** Used only to interact with the specific websites required for the extension's core features.

## 4. Data Sharing
We **do not** sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. Because we do not collect your data, there is no data to share.

## 5. Security
While all data is stored locally on your device, we recommend using standard browser security practices and keeping your browser updated to the latest version.

## 6. Compliance with Chrome Web Store Policies
The Extension complies with the **Google Single Purpose Policy** and the **User Data Policy**, including the "Limited Use" requirements.

## 7. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact the developer through the support link on the Chrome Web Store listing or via the GitHub repository.
